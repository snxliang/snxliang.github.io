Mountains and Trees - Photos from various outdoor excursions.

2025 Mt. Baker + Rainier - Summits of two of Washington's most iconic stratovolcanoes.

2024 Mt. Whitney - Took two tries via Mountaineer's Route in Spring.

2024 San Gorgonio - Overnight winter-ascent of SoCal's highest peak.

2024 Mount Baldy - Deep snow and high winds make for a fun winter-ascent of LA's highest peak.

2024 Mojave + Zion - From cowboying Kelso Dunes to post-holing up Zion's West Rim, I guess this is redemption.

2023 Death Valley - Mostly photos of Cottonwood-Marble Canyon Loop. Trail was pretty messed-up due to floods.

2023 China + HK - Guangzhou, Hong Kong, Yangshuo.

2019 Chinese Cities - Photographs from some of the biggest cities in China before the pandemic. Mostly Beijing and Shanghai but there are also a couple of Hangzhou and Nanjing.

2021 UCLA Block Party Fire - Annual tradition at the start of the school year to gather everything both flammable and not and set it on fire. Electric scooters and plastic garbage cans turned out to be not flammable nor good for the lungs. Featuring the LA Fire Department. Video of the LAFD extinguishing the fire.

2020 Demolition and Construction on Glen Drive - These houses in Coquitlam predate the high-rises and SkyTrain station that sprang up around them in the 2010s. They had been abandoned and boarded-up for years before finally being demolished for redevelopment. Simultaneously, a new high-rise was being constructed right across the street. The houses are pictured here mid-demolition and the foundation of the high-rise mid-construction using, unfortunately, my iPhone SE.

2022 San Francisco Street Performers - Portraits of street performers in San Francisco, plus some transit/airplane photos.

2021 Mulholland Drive - The iconic Mulholland Drive in Los Angeles turns out to be not very fun nor safe to walk on at night. I recommend bringing a car.

2019 Canada Day - A large singing mob formed in the aftermath of the Canada Day fireworks in Downtown Vancouver, seemingly following a man in a pikachu costume with a speaker and a large LED flagpole. They marched to the Pink Alley and proceeded to have a sort of spontaneous outdoor dance party. Shaky video of what that looked like.

2020 Henderson Place - The Coquitlam shopping mall and apartment building that was once an oasis for Cantonese-speaking diaspora in Coquitlam after the handover of Hong Kong. Pictured here 20 years later in the midst of COVID-19 lockdown measures.

2021 LAX at night - Mostly pictures of the Tom Bradley International Terminal. Featuring a crew at LAX installing high-speed internet equipment in preparation for Superbowl LVI.
